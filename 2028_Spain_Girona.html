Tour Overview

When is it?

  • The tour starts on dd.mm.yyyy so travel to Girona needs to be arranged for the previous day.
  • This tour is over a 6-day period based in Girona, riding road routes around the Spanish countryside and coastal area.
  • The tour is limited to a maximum of 7 riders.

How much will it cost?

  • For seven nights accommodation and transport of rider, bikes and luggage to and from Girona aiport, the estimated cost is around £350 per person.†
  • This estimate is based on a maximum group size of 10 riders and assumes shared twin-room accommodation.

    • †All our tours are designed to make no profit and any surplus funds are distibuted back to each rider.

What is included?

  • 7 nights' self catering accommodation in Girona.
  • Transportation of riders, bikes and luggage to and from our accommodation and Girona airport.

What is not included?

  • Personal travel and transport of bikes and luggage from UK to/from Girona airport.†
  • Cost of bike hire (if required).††
  • Meals
  • Local / Country Taxes.
  • Insurance.

    • † We’ll offer guidance on travel options and group travel arrangements.
    †† We’ll offer guidance on bike hire options.

Stages / Routes ?

Where do we ride?

The routes are designed to take you through varied landscapes, from coastal roads to rolling hills and mountain climbs.
Routes may include options to switch to a shorter alternative with less elevation, or extensions if more miles are desired.

Each day, from the following routes, a specific route will be proposed taking weather conditions into account.

Route 1: Bascara Loop

The Bàscara cycle loop is a quiet, rolling countryside ride north of Girona, known for gentle hills, medieval villages, and peaceful rural roads.

View Route

Route 2: Banyoles

This is a smooth, scenic ride that mixes quiet backroads, gentle hills, skirting Lake Banyoles — one of the most beautiful low‑intensity routes in the region.

View Route

Route 3: Rocacorba

This is a classic Girona challenge ride: a steady roll out through quiet countryside, a brutally iconic climb, and a fast, flowing return.

View Route

Route 4: Mare de Deu del Mont

This is a big, beautiful mountain day: long rolling roads, a spectacular summit climb, and panoramic views across the Pyrenees and Garrotxa.

View Route

Route 5: Coastal Loop

The coastal loop from Girona is a big, spectacular day out: rolling inland roads, a dramatic descent to the Costa Brava, cliff‑edge Mediterranean views, and a steady return through quiet countryside.

View Route

Route 6: Els Angels/Hincapie Loop

The route is a Girona classic: a smooth climb, quiet forest roads, and flowing countryside riding that feels fast, friendly, and beautifully balanced.

View Route

Route 7: Sant Hilari Sacalm Loop

The Sant Hilari Sacalm route from Girona is a long, steady mountain day with one of the region’s best sustained climbs, quiet forest roads, and a fast, flowing return.

View Route

Route 8: La porte des Pyrenees

This route is a long, scenic northern loop that gradually builds from rolling farmland into big‑mountain views, giving you a true “gateway to the Pyrenees” experience without committing to full alpine climbing.

View Route

Route 9: Costa Brava Loop

The Costa Brava loop from Girona is a spectacular day out: rolling inland roads, a dramatic descent to the Costa Brava, cliff‑edge Mediterranean views, and a steady return through quiet countryside.

View Route

Route 10: Costa Brava Loop 2

The Costa Brava loop2 from Girona is a spectacular day out: rolling inland roads, a dramatic descent to the Costa Brava, cliff‑edge Mediterranean views, and a steady return through quiet countryside.

View Route

Route 11: Girona Orbital

This is a big, mountainous adventure, combining long, steady climbs, quiet forest roads, and wide‑open views across the region.

View Route
