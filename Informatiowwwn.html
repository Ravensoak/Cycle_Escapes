Tours

Non-Profit Operation Policy

Cycle Escapes operates all tours on a non-profit basis in accordance with our founding ethos. Participant contributions are used exclusively to cover essential tour costs, including transportation and accommodation. Any surplus funds remaining after all expenses have been settled will be donated to a nominated charity. This model ensures transparency, delivers strong value for participants, and supports positive community impact.

Safety and Group Management Policy

Cycle Escapes is committed to maintaining a safe and supportive riding environment for all participants. Ride leaders are responsible for assessing conditions, managing group dynamics, and making decisions that prioritise rider safety at all times. Participants are expected to follow the instructions of ride leaders, adhere to designated routes, and ride in a manner that supports the safety and wellbeing of the group.

Booking and Participation Policy

Participation requires advance booking and full payment. Riders are responsible for ensuring they are physically prepared and that their bicycle and equipment are safe and roadworthy. Cycle Escapes reserves the right to decline participation where safety, fitness, or equipment concerns exist.

Governance and Decision-Making Policy

Cycle Escapes operates under a collaborative governance model. Ride leaders may make real-time decisions during tours to ensure safety and smooth operation. Policies are reviewed periodically to ensure alignment with our ethos and legal requirements.

Cancellation Terms Policy

Cancellations must be communicated as early as possible. Participant payments are used to secure non-refundable services, therefore refunds cannot be guaranteed. Where costs have not yet been incurred, reasonable efforts will be made to return the corresponding portion. If Cycle Escapes cancels a tour, a full refund will be provided.

Equipment Standards Policy

Riders must ensure their bicycle and equipment are safe and suitable for the route, including functioning brakes, appropriate tyres, and essential spares and tools. Participation may be declined if equipment is deemed unsafe.

Data Protection Policy

Only necessary personal data will be collected for administration, safety, and communication. Information will be stored securely, accessed only by organisers, and retained only as long as required. Data will not be shared except where essential for tour logistics.

Code of Conduct Policy

Participants are expected to behave respectfully and cooperatively, follow ride leader instructions, and support group cohesion. Behaviour that compromises safety or the ethos of Cycle Escapes may result in intervention or withdrawal from the tour.

Rider Support

Support Vehicle and Transport Policy

A designated support vehicle will transport luggage between overnight stops and provide mechanical assistance or rider pick-up if required. Participants must deliver bicycles and luggage to the specified loading point and be present for return transport at the end of the tour.

Rides

Group Riding Policy

Where practical, riders will travel as a single group. Ride leaders may divide participants into smaller ability-based groups if required. A buddy system ensures no rider is left isolated in the event of a delay or mechanical issue.

Travel

Travel Arrangements Policy

Participants are responsible for arranging their own travel to and from the start and finish points. Suggested options may be provided, and shared travel coordination will be supported where feasible.
